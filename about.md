# About Campus

## What is Campus?

Campus is a university-focused social and academic platform connecting students across **thousands of universities in Nigeria**.

Similar to Facebook or LinkedIn, Campus is a social network — but built exclusively for university students. It combines social interaction, academic collaboration, and student networking into one unified digital environment.

Every student joins their university community while also becoming part of a larger nationwide student ecosystem.

Campus is designed to become the **digital identity and collaboration space for Nigerian university students**.

---

## The Problem

University students face several challenges:

- Difficulty connecting with classmates outside physical lectures
- Limited collaboration across departments and universities
- Scattered academic discussions across multiple apps
- Lack of a student-focused social platform
- Poor access to organized study communities

Traditional social media platforms are not structured for academic life or university collaboration.

---

## Our Solution

Campus creates a structured digital environment where students automatically belong to meaningful academic communities after signup.

Students simply select:

- Their University
- Department
- Level of Study

No matriculation number is required.

Once onboarded, students gain access to their university network and the wider Nigerian student community.

---

## Who Campus Is For

Campus is built for:

- Undergraduate students
- Postgraduate students
- Student creators and innovators
- Academic communities across Nigerian universities

The platform scales across **thousands of universities nationwide**.

---

## How Campus Works

Campus operates as a **multi-university social network**.

Each university has its own community space while remaining connected to a national student network.

This ensures:

- Relevant interactions
- Organized communities
- Scalable growth
- Secure data separation between universities

---

## Technology Stack

Campus is built using modern scalable technologies:

- **Frontend:** JavaScript, HTML, CSS
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Realtime)
- **Hosting:** Vercel
- **Realtime Systems:** Supabase Realtime
- **AI Integration:** External AI APIs

This architecture enables realtime communication and rapid expansion across universities.
